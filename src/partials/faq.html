FAQ

  • How do I play Grand Cafe Story?

    Download the game from the Play Store, solve match-3 puzzles, and renovate your cafe.

  • Is the game free?

    Yes, the game is free with optional in-app purchases for boosters and additional lives.

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes, you can enjoy Grand Cafe Story without an internet connection.

  • How often is new content added?

    Regular updates bring fresh puzzles, decor, and story chapters.

  • Are there rewards for completing levels?

    Absolutely! Earn stars, coins, and exclusive items to enhance your cafe.
